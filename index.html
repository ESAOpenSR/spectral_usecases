LR vs SR Detections

A physics-constrained latent diffusion model is used to sharpen Sentinel-2 imagery while preserving spectral signatures that drive environmental analytics. The maps below show how super-resolution changes the detection of flood water (Valencia 2024) and burn scars (Palisades 2025) versus the original low-resolution inputs.

Key takeaway Spectrally constrained SR reduced false negatives/positives in water and burn-severity indices, showing that fidelity to spectra matters more than visual sharpness for operational RS pipelines.
